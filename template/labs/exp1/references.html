Earthquake Time Series Analyser

References

  1. Core Textbooks:

    • Shearer, P. M. (2009). Introduction to Seismology (2nd Edition). Cambridge University Press.

      A foundational text covering seismic waves, signal analysis, and earthquake data interpretation.

    • Lay, T., & Wallace, T. C. (1995). Modern Global Seismology. Academic Press.

      Comprehensive theory on seismic wave propagation, spectrum analysis, and global earthquake mechanisms.

  2. Digital Signal Processing Resources:

    • Oppenheim, A. V., & Schafer, R. W. (2009). Discrete-Time Signal Processing (3rd Edition). Prentice Hall.

      Essential concepts in digital signal processing, filtering, and spectral analysis.

    • Smith, S. W. (1997). The Scientist and Engineer's Guide to Digital Signal Processing.

      Practical approach to DSP concepts with applications in signal analysis.

  3. Online Resources:

    • IRIS Education and Public Outreach Program

      Educational resources and seismic data for teaching and research.

    • ObsPy Documentation

      Python framework for processing seismological data.
